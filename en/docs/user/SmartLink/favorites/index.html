To add an application to your favorites from the cards that appear on the home page, hover over an application with your mouse to display the actions, then click on the star to add it to your favorites.

You can add an application to your favorites from the search bar on the home page. To do this, search for your application by typing a part of its name or URL in the search bar, then click on the corresponding star for your application.
