✏ Rename your identities

Several Vaultys identities can be associated with your SmartLink account. You can find them in your Profile. An identity is represented by a series of numbers and letters, but it is possible to modify their names to make it more explicit for you.

1. Click on the menu​

2. Click on Profile​

3. Click on Associated VaultysIDs​

4. Click on the blue pen​

The list of your identities will then appear on the screen. Click on the blue pen of an identity to modify its name.

5. Modify the name​

In the window that appears, enter the name of your identity. For example, you can choose a name that represents the device on which your identity is stored.

Finally, click on Save.
